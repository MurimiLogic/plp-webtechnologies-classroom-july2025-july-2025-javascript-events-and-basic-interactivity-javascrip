Karibu!

Nairobi Street Eats brings the authentic flavors of Kenya's vibrant street food scene right to your neighborhood. Founded by siblings Wanjiku and Kamau Mwangi, our food truck celebrates the rich culinary traditions passed down through generations.

Wanjiku learned to cook at her grandmother's side in Eastleigh, mastering the art of Kenyan street food. She later refined her skills at the prestigious Utalii College. Kamau, with his passion for fresh ingredients, sources directly from local markets, just like the vibrant open-air markets of Nairobi.

Now experience the taste of Kenya on wheels! We've partnered with Maasai Mara Grill to bring you the most authentic East African cuisine, prepared with the same care and attention to detail you'd find in the heart of Nairobi.
