Catering

About Our Mobile Kitchen

After years of dreaming and saving, Nairobi Street Eats hit the road with our custom-built food truck that we designed specifically for authentic Kenyan street food preparation. Our kitchen was crafted with both tradition and modernity in mind.

The 22' mobile kitchen allows us to bring the vibrant tastes of Nairobi to any location, from large corporate events to intimate private parties. We can set up anywhere with just a parking space and bring the authentic Kenyan food experience directly to you.

Our mobile restaurant is perfect for catering your wedding, event, or party in Nairobi. We don't just deliver - we prepare your food with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and serve it with the authentic flavors of Kenyan street food. Nairobi Street Eats is ideal for venues that want to offer something truly special.

Consider us for:

  • Weddings & Harambees
  • Corporate Events
  • Sports Events
  • Birthday Parties
  • Community Gatherings
  • Cultural Festivals
... and much more!

Choose from our standard catering menu or contact us to create a customized menu for your special occasion. We're flexible and dedicated to making your event unforgettable with authentic Kenyan cuisine.
